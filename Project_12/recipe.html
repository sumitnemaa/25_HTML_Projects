Delicious Choclate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In another bowl, beat melted butter, brown sugar, and white sugar.
  3. Stir in vanilla, egg, and egg yolk.
  4. Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips and scoop dough onto baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until edges are golden brown.
  7. Let cool before serving.
  8. In a bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
